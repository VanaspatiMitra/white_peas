About the Crop:

White Peas (Matar) - PUSA 105 is a high-yielding variety known for its quality and adaptability. It thrives in well-drained, loamy to sandy loam soils with a pH of 6.0 to 7.5. The crop requires about 500-700 mm of water per acre, supplied through rainfall or irrigation. Typically, PUSA 105 produces around 700-900 kg of peas per acre. Profit margins are generally favorable, ranging from 20-30%, depending on input costs and market prices.

  • Soil Composition: Well-drained, loamy to sandy loam soil. pH range of 6.0 to 7.5
  • Water need per Acre: 1.64 to 2.30feet
  • Potential Dangers: Soil Degradation, Pest Infestation, Waterlogging
  • Fertilizers: Diammonium Phosphate, Organic Matter, Potassium, Nitrogen
  • Pesticides: Carbaryl,Thiamethoxam, Acetamiprid, Sulphur
